** Badge It -  Current Development and Ethics of AI Group Project
:PROPERTIES:
:HTML_CONTAINER_CLASS: badge
:END:
- *Green pen Matters*
 
*** For this activity you need to work in a group 2-3 to produce a final presentation. The process is as following:
 
  - Pick one current AI technology (see the list for suggestions below)
  - Answer the default set of questions
  - Pick three questions, two if only two members in a team from a given list as your project focus. 
  - Work out who is responsible for which questions in your team
  - Brain storm and pick a presentation format to present your work
  - Decide who is going to present to the whole group upon finish
*** Organise your project
- Use [[./doc/AI-Group-Project.docx][this project booklet]] to organise your team and your work
- Using those two articles to get some ideas:
  1. Here is an [[http://beebom.com/2015/12/examples-of-artificial-intelligence][article]].
  2. Here is another [[https://emerj.com/ai-sector-overviews/everyday-examples-of-ai/][article]] 
*** Assessment of your team work
- Your team work will be awarded Silver, Gold or Platinum badges depending on the overall quality of your work.
